üí¨ STAR Method for Interviews

  • S - Situation: Set the context
  • T - Task: Describe your responsibility
  • A - Action: Explain what you did
  • R - Result: Share the outcome
  • Be specific with numbers/metrics
  • Keep answers 2-3 minutes
  • Practice common questions aloud
  • Prepare 5-6 stories covering different skills
